Village Culture & Festivals

Villages are the soul of our culture. From colorful festivals to age-old traditions, every event is a reflection of community, simplicity, and deep-rooted happiness.

Dasera Celebration
"Desera celebrations in our village!The drums beat, the rituals unfold, and the spirit of tradition comes alive. A time for community, culture, and joy!

Raja Festival
"Raja Festival vibes in our village! Celebrating the onset of monsoon and the beauty of womanhood, with traditional rituals, folk music, and delicious local cuisine. A time for community, culture, and joy! "

Diwali in Village
"Village Diwali vibes! Rural charm meets festive glow. Diyas lighting up homes, sweets being shared, and loved ones coming together. A celebration like no other!

Holi – Festival of Colors
"Colours of joy! Celebrating Holi in our village with vibrant hues, traditional dances, and sweet treats. A time for love, laughter, and new beginnings!
