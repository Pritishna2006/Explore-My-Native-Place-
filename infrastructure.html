Infrastructure & Facilities

Infrastructure is the backbone of rural development. Our village is steadily evolving with improved roads, better healthcare, reliable electricity, and clean water facilities.

Paved Roads

Well-constructed roads connect our village to nearby towns, improving trade, travel, and communication.

Healthcare Center

A modern healthcare center ensures medical support for all, with regular health camps and trained staff.

Water & Sanitation

Clean drinking water, public toilets, and sanitation awareness have greatly improved hygiene and health.

Electricity

Our village is electrified, supporting homes, schools, and businesses, and boosting evening productivity.
